About Me:

{% include smallcaps.html word="I'm Jackson Daumeyer," %} a Computer Science Student with a passion for design studying at The Ohio State University. My passion for design started when I read The Design of Everyday Things by Dan Norman. The stories of how bad design could influence people's lives really stuck with me and I knew from that day that I would work to make everything I can more accessible. I now carry this passion into every new project I take.
